#+SETUPFILE: ~/.emacs.d/org-templates/template.org
#+TITLE: picture mode

* 快捷键
1. C-c C-c: 退出picture-mode
2. C-c ^(picture-movement-up): 画图方向设为向上
3. C-c .(picture-movement-down): 向下
4. C-c >(picture-movement-right): 向右
5. C-c <(picture-movement-left): 向左
6. C-c `(picture-movement-nw): 西北
7. C-c '(picture-movement-ne): 东北
8. C-c /(picture-movement-sw): 西南
9. C-c \(picture-movement-se): 东南
